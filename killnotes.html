KILL NOTES

A MUSICAL ELIMINATION GAME


Kill Notes is a minimalist strategy game featuring a clean 2D geometric aesthetic. The core gameplay focuses on tactical decision-making: players must strategically switch between various weapons each turn to eliminate waves of advancing enemies before they reach the finish line.


The game is enhanced by a dynamic auditory layer where each enemy emits a distinct musical pitch based on its remaining health. While the strategy remains the priority, this audio feedback creates a unique, evolving soundscape as you combat the threat. Kill Notes offers a precise and focused strategic experience for those who appreciate functional design and calculated action.
